Introduction:

This HIT is just to inform you about the payment and bonus possibilities for a series of HITs that we will be releasing to a select group of workers over the coming weeks. You do not have to do anything other than indicate that you have read this information.

If you need to access this information after completing this HIT, you can view a copy of all of this info here. You are welcome to bookmark the page or download a copy for your own reference.


General information

Welcome! This information is to help you understand the overall layout of the study and mainly provides information on (i) the tasks to be completed and (ii) the payment & bonus structure. We will send out an email each week when we release a new batch of HITs.

Tasks

In each writing task (= 1 HIT), you will be asked to read a line from an unfamiliar text and write three sentences that relate to it. Using only this text and what you know about the world:

  • Write one sentence that is deﬁnitely correct about the situation or event in the line.
  • Write one sentence that might be correct about the situation or event in the line.
  • Write one sentence that is deﬁnitely incorrect about the situation or event in the line.

Each week, there will also be a categorization task (as validation). More information on both these tasks will be provided to you in a FAQ document linked in each HIT.

  • Bonuses 1-3 are determined based on how many HITs you complete throughout the week.
  • Bonus 4 is awarded if you’ve completed more than 25 HITs and your responses on 95% of the writing HITs are validated by a majority of other workers.
